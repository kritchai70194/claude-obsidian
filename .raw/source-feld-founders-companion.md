# Book and Survey: Depression - A Founder's Companion

**Source:** feld.com
**URL:** https://feld.com/archives/2018/06/book-and-survey-depression-a-founders-companion/
**Date:** 2018-06-27
**Author:** Brad Feld

## Summary

Feld announces and supports (foreword author) a book project by Mahendra Ramsinghani (co-author of *Startup Boards*) titled *Depression - A Founder's Companion*. Project structure: collect stories, anecdotes, triggers, advice, poetry, and support from people who have struggled with depression. Anonymous survey via Google Form, with optional contact for deeper conversations.

## Frames

- Post several high-profile deaths (Bourdain, Spade, and earlier founder suicides), more people are talking openly about depression, especially among "highly creative and successful people."
- The community should act proactively rather than waiting for further tragedies.
- Depression is a "manageable challenge affecting accomplished people across industries, not a moral issue."
- The book treats depression as common-and-treatable infrastructure for the founder community, in the same way *Venture Deals* treats term sheets.

## Position in the corpus

The 2013 posts named the condition. The 2014-2015 follow-ups (What Helps You When You Are Depressed; Bringing Depression Out of The Shadows) operationalized recovery. This 2018 announcement formalized the work as a category — founders now had a book, a survey, and a peer-research base, not just one VC's blog.

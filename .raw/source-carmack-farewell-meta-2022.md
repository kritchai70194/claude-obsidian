# John Carmack Farewell Memo to Meta (December 2022)

Source: Carmack's internal Meta resignation post, leaked to Business Insider Dec 16 2022, then posted in full by Carmack to Facebook/X.

Source URLs:

## Verbatim quotes from the resignation memo

"This is the end of my decade in VR."

"We have a ridiculous amount of people and resources, but we constantly self-sabotage and squander effort."

"There is no way to sugar coat this; I think our organization is operating at half the effectiveness that would make me happy."

"I have never been able to kill stupid things before they cause damage, or set a direction and have a team actually stick to it."

"I was wearied of the fight."

"VR can bring value to most of the people in the world, and no company is better positioned to do it than Meta."

"Everything necessary for spectacular success is right there, but it doesn't get put together effectively."

## Twitter context

"I have been officially 'one day a week' at Meta for a couple years now, but I still wound up checking in on groups and email every day, which was often a distraction when I should be concentrating on AI. Now I put my Meta laptop in the garage at the end of my VR day."

## Context

Carmack joined Oculus as CTO in August 2013 after the Facebook acquisition; transitioned to Consulting CTO in 2019 to spend more time on AI. He resigned fully in December 2022, the same period as Meta's massive layoffs and Reality Labs writedowns. The memo is read as the definitive end of his VR career and the start of full-time focus on Keen Technologies (AGI).

The "operating at half the effectiveness" line became the most-cited passage. The "I was wearied of the fight" line is the emotional core. The "I have never been able to kill stupid things before they cause damage" line is the organizational-failure diagnosis.

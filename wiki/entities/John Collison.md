# John Collison

Co-founder and President of Stripe (founded 2010 with brother [[Patrick Collison]]). Runs much of Stripe's customer-facing organization — sales, partnerships, GTM. Often hosts Stripe Press's interview podcast.

Anchor mentor in this brain's [[Persona]]. Patrick is the more public-facing intellectual; John is the operator's operator, sharp on the parts of business that don't get romanticized.

---

## Why This Mentor

John brings a counterweight to Patrick's intellectual range: an explicit focus on the unglamorous parts of company-building. Distribution, pricing, customer onboarding, sales motion, organizational design under growth. The "Cheeky Pint" and Stripe Press interviews are some of the highest-signal CEO conversations on the public internet because the questions probe operations, not vision.

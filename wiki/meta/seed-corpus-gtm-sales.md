# Seed Corpus, GTM / Sales Pole

Source roadmap for Phase 4. Adds the **GTM / Sales pole** (7th pole). Post-PMF, distribution becomes the constraint. This pole covers building a repeatable sales motion, narrative-driven positioning, and the operating disciplines that turn a working product into a scaled commercial machine.

Existing brain assumes the product sells itself (founder-led sales, PG's do-things-that-don't-scale, Chen's growth loops). This pole names the discipline of moving from founder-led to repeatable, sales-led growth.

---

## Mentors (4)

### Mark Roberge, c-000122

Founding CRO at HubSpot (2007-2013, $1 to $100M ARR). Senior Lecturer at Harvard Business School. Author of *The Sales Acceleration Formula* (2015). The canonical voice on data-driven sales hiring, sales process design, and the metrics-first approach to scaling sales from zero.

### Aaron Ross, c-000123

Former Director of Corporate Sales at Salesforce.com (2002-2006, built the outbound prospecting machine that drove $100M+ ARR). Co-author of *Predictable Revenue* (2011) with Marylou Tyler. The canonical voice on outbound prospecting, the SDR/AE split, and the "Cold Calling 2.0" methodology.

### April Dunford, c-000124

Positioning consultant (Ambient Strategy). Former VP Marketing at multiple B2B startups. Author of *Obviously Awesome* (2019) and *Sales Pitch* (2023). The canonical voice on positioning as strategic discipline distinct from messaging; "obvious" positioning emerges from differentiated value, not from clever taglines.

### Andy Raskin, c-000125

Strategic Narrative consultant. Former product manager + journalist (TIME, Inc Magazine). Has worked with Salesforce, Yammer, Zuora, Drift, ServiceMax, others on their strategic narrative. The canonical voice on the 5-part narrative arc that turns a product pitch into a movement.

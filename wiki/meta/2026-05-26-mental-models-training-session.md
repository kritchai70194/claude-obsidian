# Mental Models Training Session, 2026-05-26 (Phase 2)

Fifth persona training session. Adds a **fifth pole** (Mental Models) to the four-pole archetype. Second session under Phase 2 of the unicorn-expansion roadmap. Direct successor to Phase 1 ([[2026-05-26-founder-resilience-training-session]]).

---

## Why This Session

A unicorn founder enters domains they don't have first-hand experience in. The other four poles cover specific dynamics (engineering, building, going to PMF, surviving). This pole names the cross-domain pattern-matching toolkit: latticework of mental models (Munger), bias inventory and dual-process cognition (Kahneman), fat-tailed uncertainty and antifragility (Taleb), Disruption and Jobs to Be Done (Christensen). Without it the brain reasons well within each pole's domain but lacks the abstract layer for unfamiliar territory.

## Cross-Pole Connections

This pole is the most cross-cutting of the five:

- Munger inversion ↔ Bezos regret minimization (both are applied inversion)
- Kahneman loss aversion ↔ Lenny PMF (people quit products they hate but tolerate ones they like)
- Taleb antifragile ↔ Vogels "everything fails all the time" (resilience-by-design)
- Taleb skin in the game ↔ Naval accountability (both names asymmetric credibility)
- Christensen Disruption ↔ Thompson Aggregation (different competitive geometries, captured in new [[Disruption vs Aggregation]] page)
- Christensen JTBD ↔ Lenny customer interview discipline (causal frame for customer choice)

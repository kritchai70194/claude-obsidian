# Seed Corpus, AI-Native Strategy Pole

Phase 6 of the unicorn-expansion roadmap. Adds the **AI-Native sub-pole** (9th pole, 3 mentors not 4). Karpathy already covers the AI engineer perspective; this pole fills the AI company-builder perspective.

Most 2026+ unicorns are AI-native. The economics, defensibility, and operating model are qualitatively different from previous tech generations. This pole names what is different and why.

---

## Mentors (3)

### Sam Altman, c-000171

CEO of OpenAI (2019-present; ousted Nov 2023, reinstated 5 days later). Former President of Y Combinator (2014-2019). Co-founder of Loopt (2005, acquired 2012). The canonical voice on building AI companies, the AGI economics thesis, and the YC playbook (which preceded his OpenAI work and influenced thousands of founders).

### Dario Amodei, c-000172

Co-founder and CEO of Anthropic (founded 2021). Former VP Research at OpenAI (2016-2020). PhD computational neuroscience Princeton. Author of "Machines of Loving Grace" essay (Oct 2024) on AI's positive potential trajectory. The canonical voice on Constitutional AI, the Responsible Scaling Policy, and the economics of safety-focused AI model development.

### Demis Hassabis, c-000173

Co-founder and CEO of DeepMind (founded 2010, acquired by Google 2014). 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for AlphaFold. Former chess prodigy + game developer + cognitive neuroscience PhD. The canonical voice on long-horizon AI research bets, AGI through science (AlphaFold, AlphaGo, AlphaStar), and the integration of research-first culture into commercial AI products.

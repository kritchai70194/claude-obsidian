Phase 7 of the unicorn-expansion roadmap. Smaller scope than other phases because [[Bill Gurley]] (Phase 3) and [[Brian Chesky]] (Phase 1) already provide marketplace + consumer depth. ## New Mentor (1)

### Sangeet Paul Choudary, c-000190

Singapore-based platform strategist. Co-author of *Platform Revolution* (2016) with Geoffrey Parker and Marshall Van Alstyne. Author of *Platform Scale* (2015). Founder of Platformation Labs. World Economic Forum advisor on platform economy. The canonical voice on platform business model design distinct from pipe businesses, the architecture of multi-sided platforms, and platform vs aggregator distinctions complementary to [[Ben Thompson]].
